Display Cabinets

We use high quality acrylic in our display cabinets to ensure that we acheve a premium finish - clear acrylic display cases are as optically clear as glass.
Acrylic is often used in retail areas or public areas due to its safe properties - it will not shatter on impact like glass. All of our acrylic display cabinets have the option of having lighting. We frequently add low energy LED lights or track spotlights in a display case.
Sasec also sell a range of glass display cabinets with aluminium finish or black framework.
