Lecterns

Acrylic lecterns are a popular choice for many , lecture halls, conferences or presentations. The modern, minimalist design of our lecterns makes them perfect for any modern venue while the transparency of the material allows them to work just as well in a more traditional setting.

Lecterns

What are the benefits of an Acrylic Lectern ?

As well as the great designs, acrylic lecterns offer a number of practical benefits. They are easily cleaned and maintained, relatively light and easy to customise.

Our fantastic range includes something to suit every taste and budget.
