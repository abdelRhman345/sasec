Retail & Point Of Sale

Standard point of sale display products include poster holders, menu holders, table talkers, leaflet dispensers and promotional blocks.
There are also a selection of acrylic blocks, suggestion boxes, collection boxes, display bridges or display steps for retail point of sale displays and window areas.
This category also includes some more unusual products such as the encapsulated branding blocks, which are becoming extremely popular for gift presentations or awards.
Please email or phone if you have an enquiry for a volume order as we aim to deliver a competitive quotation upon request.

As the manufacturer of these products we are also able to apply Screenprinting and branding to any of these display items if required.
