Home & Office

Sasec design and manufacture acrylic or Perspex furniture. We have a range of acrylic coffee tables, console table designs, book shelves and cabinets.

Home & Office

Luminati also have a range of Lifestyle home or office accessories to compliment our acrylic furniture range.
The Luminati lifestyle products include:

TV stands and risers Magazine racks DVD storage racks Trays and Acessories Guitar Stands and wall hangers and much much more...

Our clear acrylic furniture is extremely popular as it has a modern design and fits beautifully into any home or office. The transparency of the Perspex furniture is perfect for allowing clear visibility and allowing light through - perfect for smaller rooms.
The addition of black or white acrylic or engraving to our acrylic tables enhance their modern look.
All of our acrylic furniture is hand finished and polished to achieve a superior look.
